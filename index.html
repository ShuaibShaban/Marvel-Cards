Marvel Characters

Iron Man

Iron Man is the superhero persona of Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark, a businessman and engineer who runs the weapons manufacturing company Stark Industries.

Thor

Thor is the Asgardian God of Thunder and has god-like strength, durability, and agility. He can also call down lightning and thunder, and wields the mystical war hammer Mjolnir.

Hulk

The Hulk was one of the first five founding heroes of the Avengers and served as the team's main weapon. He is the deuteragonist of the Reassemble campaign in Marvel's Avengers.
